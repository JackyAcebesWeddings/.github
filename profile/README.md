## About

**Cebu Weddings & Events Planner by Jacky Acebes** is a luxury boutique wedding
planning and coordination service based in Cebu, Philippines. With **11+ years**
of experience and proud membership in the **Philippine Association of Wedding
Planners (PAWP)**, Jacky and her team bring elegant, stress-free celebrations to
life — from intimate ceremonies to destination weddings, debuts, galas, and
corporate events.

## Services

- 💍 **Wedding Planning & Coordination** — full-service and day-of coordination
- 🎉 **Debut & Milestone Events** — bespoke celebrations and themed galas
- 🌿 **Decor & Styling** — floral design, venue styling, and curated palettes
- 🤝 **Vendor Curation** — a trusted network of premium Cebu suppliers
- 🌎 **Destination Weddings** — local and overseas event coordination
